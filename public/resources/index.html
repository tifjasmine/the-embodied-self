A gentle note

Resources can support the work, but they do not have to become another thing to perfect.

Use what feels helpful. Leave what does not. Come back to the same worksheet more than once. Let your answers change. Healing is not about doing everything right — it is about building a more honest, compassionate relationship with yourself over time.

Need more support?

You do not have to figure it all out alone.

Worksheets and articles can be a beautiful place to begin. Therapy offers a space to go deeper, feel supported, and make sense of what is coming up with someone in your corner.
